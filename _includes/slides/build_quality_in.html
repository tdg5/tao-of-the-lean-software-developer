Sandi Metz from Practical Object-Oriented Development in Ruby

Two kinds of integrity

Perceived integrity means that the totality of the product achieves a balance of function, usability, reliability, and economy that delights customers.

Conceptual integrity means that the system's central concepts work together as a smooth, cohesive whole.

Maintaining Conceptual Integrity

  1. Simplicity
  2. Clarity
  3. Suitabiity for use
  4. No repetition
  5. No extra features

Resist the temptation of the brute force approach and add the architectural capability. If you let crud build up in the system, its integrity will begin to degrade, and you will eventually have to pay the debt.
